\begin{abstract}
Social security is an essential element in the economic and political development of societies. 

Pension is one of the biggest social impacts.
Many implemented systems have some problems.



The aim of this paper is to examine a Layer-2 solution for optimal scalability while maintaining all the benefits of blockchain technology regarding decentralized social security systems.
