\begin{abstract}
Social security is an essential element in the economic and political development of societies. 

Pension is one of the biggest social impacts.
Many implemented systems have some problems.



The aim of this paper is to examine a decentralized pension solution wich is transparent, fair and accessable around the world with  all the benefits of blockchain technology.

\end{abstract}
